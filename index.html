How to Install & Maintain

Just place RBCCD.exe to any empty folder on your computer and run. Window disappears after 10 seconds — before it you can cancel program loading by pressing Ctrl-C or closing window. To see the state of the Converter, right click on the tray icon (green cat) and select "Show log window". To shutdown program, select "Shutdown Converter" from the same menu. RIBCE converter did not autostart after computer reboot, you must run it manually.

System Requirements

Windows 7 or 8/8.1 with Microsoft Office (2010 - 2013) Word installed. 2 Gb RAM and 4 Gb free space on hard disk.
